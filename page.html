Introduction to Cats

Cats are one of the most popular pets in the world. They are known for their independent nature and playful behavior.

Cat Characteristics

Physical Attributes

Cats have sharp claws and teeth, which help them hunt. They also have excellent night vision.

Behavior

Cats are known to be meticulous groomers. They also communicate through meowing, purring, and hissing.

Diet

Cats are carnivores and primarily eat meat. A balanced diet is essential for their health.

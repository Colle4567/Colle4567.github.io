Why I like cats.

Cats are one of the most popular pets in the world. They are known for their independent nature and playful behavior. Thats why I like cats. Cats should be liked. Not liking a cat is like not liking yourself.

Cat Characteristics

Physical Attributes

Cats have sharp claws and teeth, which help them hunt. They also have excellent night vision.

Behavior

Kneading

Kneading. One of the most common and distinctive catlike displays of affection is kneading.

Chattering

When your cat sees a fly or some other potential prey that she can't catch.

Scratching

Cats like to scratch. They do it to play, stretch, sharpen their claws, and mark their territory.

Diet

Cats are carnivores and primarily eat meat. A balanced diet is essential for their health.
